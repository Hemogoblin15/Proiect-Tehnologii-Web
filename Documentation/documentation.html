Introduction

Manners Matter! aims to create a responsive web application based on a service-oriented architecture that supports learning and recommending norms and rules of behavior (etiquette) on the internet. The application will offer practical advice on how to behave in different online contexts and events, including academic, work-related, family-related, and group-related situations, among others. The recommendations will be tailored to each user's profile, taking into account demographic factors, professional training, social status, culture, and other relevant information.

The proposed project will contribute to improving online behavior and promoting digital etiquette by providing practical advice to users on how to interact respectfully and appropriately in different online environments.

Methodology

The methodology of this project involves using HTML and CSS for the frontend development, which will be responsible for creating an attractive and user-friendly interface for the web application. Node.js will be used for backend development, providing the necessary server-side functionality to support the service-based architecture. The system will be designed to suggest relevant and practical behavioral advice based on the user profile and the type of online context/event. To achieve this, the system will monitor the progress made by each user in the learning process, which may be structured by levels of difficulty. Basic functionalities will also be provided through a Web REST/GraphQL API. Additionally, the system will generate a ranking of the most successful users, which will be available as an RSS data feed. By following this methodology, we aim to deliver a comprehensive and effective web application that will support learning and recommending norms and rules of behavior on the internet.

Mission

At Manners Matter!, we believe that good etiquette is the foundation of healthy online interactions. Our website provides support for learning and recommending norms and rules of behavior (etiquette) on the internet. Our mission is to promote healthy and respectful online communication by providing educational resources and practical tips on internet etiquette. We believe that everyone has a role to play in creating a safe and positive online environment.

Discussion

Manners, or social etiquette, are a set of unwritten rules that guide our interactions with others. They encompass everything from saying "please" and "thank you" to how we dress and behave in public. While some may argue that manners are simply a form of social conditioning or unnecessary formalities, there are several reasons why manners matter.

Firstly, manners help to establish respect and consideration for others. When we use polite language and show good manners, we demonstrate that we value the people we are interacting with and are considerate of their feelings. This, in turn, helps to build positive relationships with others and can lead to better communication and cooperation.

Secondly, manners can help to avoid misunderstandings and conflicts. By following social etiquette, we are able to communicate our intentions clearly and avoid causing offense or confusion. This is especially important in today's diverse and multicultural society where people come from different backgrounds and have different cultural norms.

Lastly, good manners can help to boost our own self-confidence and self-esteem. When we know how to behave in different social situations, we feel more comfortable and confident in our interactions with others. This can lead to increased social opportunities, career success, and a general sense of well-being.

In summary, manners matter because they help us to show respect and consideration for others, avoid misunderstandings and conflicts, and boost our own self-confidence and self-esteem. By following social etiquette, we can create a more harmonious and respectful society where everyone feels valued and appreciated.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the web application we aim to develop has the potential to be a valuable tool for anyone who wishes to improve their online communication skills and manners. With the increasing reliance on digital communication, the importance of proper etiquette on the internet cannot be overstated. By providing practical advice and information tailored to different contexts and audiences, our application can empower users to communicate more effectively and appropriately online. Additionally, the monitoring and feedback features can help users track their progress and encourage them to continue developing their skills. Ultimately, we believe that this application has the potential to enhance online communication and promote a more respectful and inclusive online community.
