About Us

At Manners Matter!, we believe that good etiquette is the foundation of healthy online interactions. Our website provides support for learning and recommending norms and rules of behavior (etiquette) on the internet.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote healthy and respectful online communication by providing educational resources and practical tips on internet etiquette. We believe that everyone has a role to play in creating a safe and positive online environment.
